About

Welcome to POPPED! A tool and archive website for everything popmarts and vinyl figures. Dive into our vibe with an archive that feels like a backstage pass to vinyl figure culture. With a laid-back archive, we are packed with cool details about each figure, its artist, and every series release.

Keep track of your prized finds with the Collection Tracker, and let the Series Random Generator spark some gambling magic. At POPPED!, it's not just a website, it's a community of collectors and enthusiasts here to celebrate and have fun with all things vinyl. Enjoy building and expanding your own pop page! Let's get POPPIN!!!
